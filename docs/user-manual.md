## Overview

Hellenic Directory of America is a private community platform for the Greek Orthodox community in North America. It has two modes:

- **Public access** — anyone can search parishes and businesses without an account
- **Member access** — registered users join invite-only community directories, message other members, and manage a profile

You can use the platform on:
- **Web**: [hellenicdir.com](https://hellenicdir.com) in any modern browser
- **iOS**: Download from the App Store (requires iPhone or iPad running iOS 16.0+)
- **Android**: Download from Google Play (requires Android 8.0+)

## Account Registration

### Who Can Register?

Anyone can create a free account. Your account lets you join community directories when invited, message other members, and manage a profile.

### How to Register

**Web:**
1. Go to [hellenicdir.com/register](https://hellenicdir.com/register)
2. Enter your full name, email address, password, and optionally your phone number
3. Check all three consent boxes:
   - **Privacy Policy** — confirms you have read and agree to how your data is used
   - **Terms of Service** — confirms you agree to the platform rules
   - **Sensitive Data Consent** — required because information about your Greek Orthodox community affiliation is classified as sensitive personal data under US, Canadian, and Quebec privacy law
4. Click **Create Account**
5. Check your email for a verification link and click it to activate your account

**iOS:**
1. Open the app — you will be taken to the sign-in screen
2. Tap **Don't have an account? Register**
3. Fill in your details and check all three consent boxes
4. Tap **Create Account**

**Android:**
1. Open the app
2. Tap **Don't have an account? Register**
3. Fill in your details and check all three consent boxes
4. Tap **Create Account**

### Password Requirements

- Minimum 8 characters
- We recommend using a mix of upper- and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols
- Do not reuse passwords from other services

### Email Verification

After registering, you will receive a verification email. Click the link in the email within 24 hours. If you do not verify your email, some features may be restricted.

If you did not receive the verification email:
- Check your spam folder
- Make sure you entered the correct email address

## Signing In

**Web:**
1. Go to [hellenicdir.com/login](https://hellenicdir.com/login)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

**iOS/Android:**
1. Open the app
2. Enter your email and password
3. Tap **Sign In**

### Staying Signed In

Your session lasts up to 30 days. On mobile, you remain signed in between app launches. On web, your session is maintained via secure cookies.

If you are signed out unexpectedly, your session may have expired for security reasons. Sign in again.

### Forgotten Password

A password reset flow is available from the sign-in screen. Enter your email and you will receive a reset link.

## Parishes

The parish directory is public — you do not need an account to use it.

### Searching for Parishes

**Web:**
1. Click **Parishes** in the navigation
2. Enter a city, state abbreviation, or zip code in the search bar
3. Results show matching parishes sorted by proximity

**Mobile:**
1. Tap **Parishes** in the bottom navigation bar
2. Type in the search field to filter by location

### Parish Information

Each parish listing shows:
- Name and jurisdiction (e.g., Greek Orthodox Archdiocese of America)
- Address and contact information
- Clergy (priest, deacon)
- Service times (when available)
- Interactive map showing the parish location

### Map View

Tap the map pin on a parish card to see its location. On iOS, the parish detail page includes a full MapKit map you can pan and zoom.

### Data Source

Parish data is sourced from the Greek Orthodox Archdiocese of America (GOARCH) and updated automatically each day at 2:00 AM UTC.

## Business Directory

The business directory lists Greek-owned and Greek-operated businesses across North America. It is public — no account required.

### Searching for Businesses

**Web:**
1. Click **Businesses** in the navigation
2. Use the search bar to search by business name, keyword, or category
3. Use the location filter to narrow by city or state

**Mobile:**
1. Tap **Businesses** in the bottom navigation

### Business Listings

Each listing shows:
- Business name and logo
- Category (restaurant, law firm, real estate, etc.)
- City and state
- Website, phone, and email (if provided)
- Description

Businesses are reviewed and approved by platform administrators before appearing in the directory.

## Community Directories

A community directory is a private, invite-only group of people. Think of it as a members-only address book for a specific community — a parish, a professional network, an AHEPA chapter, a university group, or a family association.

### Joining a Directory

You can only join a directory when an existing member or administrator sends you an invitation. There is no way to request to join on your own.

**When you receive an invitation:**
1. Check your email for an invitation from Hellenic Directory
2. Click the link in the email
3. If you already have an account, you will be asked to accept or decline
4. If you do not have an account, you will be taken through registration first

### Viewing Your Directories

After signing in, your dashboard shows all directories you belong to. Click or tap any directory to open the member list.

### Browsing Members

Inside a directory, you can:
- Browse all members
- Search members by name, city, employer, or industry
- View a member's profile card showing their contact preferences and organization affiliations

### Organization Affiliations

Members can self-report affiliations with Greek Orthodox organizations (AHEPA, Philoptochos, GOYA, etc.). Verified affiliations — confirmed by a directory administrator — are shown with a badge.

## Messaging

The messaging system is private and only available to authenticated members within a directory.

### Reading Messages

**Web:**
1. Open a directory
2. Click **Messages** in the directory navigation
3. The left panel shows your message threads
4. Click a thread to read it

**Mobile:**
1. Open a directory
2. Tap the envelope icon
3. Tap a thread to open it

### Sending a Message

1. Open a thread or start a new one
2. Type your message in the text field at the bottom
3. Press Send (or tap the send icon on mobile)

### Types of Messages

- **Direct threads**: one-to-one or small group conversations between members
- **Broadcast messages**: sent by directory administrators to all members simultaneously; you can read but not reply to broadcasts

### Email Notifications

When you receive a message, the platform will email you a notification if you are not currently active in the app. Reply via the app rather than directly to the notification email.

## Your Profile

### Editing Your Profile

**Web:**
1. Click your name or avatar in the navigation
2. Go to **Profile**
3. Edit your display name, phone, city, employer, industry, and profile photo

**Mobile:**
1. Tap **Profile** in the bottom navigation
2. Tap the edit icon

### Profile Information

Your profile within a directory includes:
- Full name and profile photo
- City and state (general location — not your full address)
- Employer and industry (optional)
- Organization affiliations (optional)
- Contact preferences

This information is visible only to other members of the same directory. It is not publicly visible on the web.

### Organization Affiliations

To add an affiliation:
1. Go to your profile
2. Tap or click **Add Affiliation**
3. Select the organization from the list
4. The affiliation will show as "pending" until verified by a directory administrator

## Account Settings & Data Rights

### Accessing Account Settings

**Web:** Click your avatar → **Account Settings**

**iOS/Android:** Tap **Profile** → **Account Settings** (or scroll to the bottom of the profile screen)

### Downloading Your Data

Under CCPA §1798.110 and PIPEDA, you have the right to a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click or tap **Download My Data**
3. A JSON file containing your profile, directory memberships, message history, and consent records will download immediately

### Deleting Your Account

Under CCPA §1798.105 and GDPR Article 17, you have the right to have your data deleted.

**Web:**
1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Scroll to **Delete Account**
3. Click **Delete My Account**
4. Read the consequences (listed clearly)
5. Confirm by typing your email address in the confirmation box
6. Click **Permanently Delete**

**iOS:**
1. Go to **Profile**
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Read the warning
5. Tap **Continue**, then **Delete My Account** to confirm

**Android:**
1. Go to **Profile**
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Tap **Continue** in the first dialog, then **Delete My Account** in the confirmation dialog

**What happens when you delete your account:**
- Your account is deactivated immediately
- You are signed out on all devices
- All your personal data is permanently deleted after 30 days
- Directory memberships and messages are removed
- This action cannot be reversed after the 30-day grace period

If you change your mind within 30 days, contact support at [support@hellenicdir.com](mailto:support@hellenicdir.com).

## Directory Administrators

Directory administrators manage the membership and communications within a specific directory.

### Inviting Members

**Web:**
1. Open the directory
2. Click the **Invite** button (person-plus icon) at the top right
3. Enter the email address of the person you want to invite
4. Click **Send Invitation**

The invitee will receive an email. They must accept the invitation before appearing in the member list. This two-step confirmation is intentional — no one is added to a directory without their explicit acceptance.

**Mobile:**
1. Open the directory
2. Tap the person-plus icon
3. Enter the email address
4. Tap **Send**

### Removing Members

1. Open the directory member list
2. Find the member you want to remove
3. Tap their name to open their profile
4. Tap or click **Remove from Directory**
5. Confirm the action

### Sending a Broadcast Message

A broadcast message is sent to all members of the directory at once.

1. Open the directory
2. Go to **Messages**
3. Click or tap **New Broadcast**
4. Write your message
5. Send — all members receive it as a notification and in their inbox

### Verifying Organization Affiliations

When a member adds a self-reported organization affiliation, it appears as "pending" until an administrator verifies it.

1. Go to **Directory Settings** or the member's profile
2. Find pending affiliation requests
3. Click **Verify** to confirm or **Decline** to remove the pending request

## Platform Owners & App Admins

Platform owners and admins have access to the admin dashboard at `/admin`. This panel is only visible to users with the `OWNER` or `ADMIN` app role.

### User Management

- View all registered users
- Change a user's app role (REGISTERED → ADMIN, or demote)
- All role changes are recorded in the immutable audit log with the acting admin's ID, timestamp, IP address, and user-agent

### Directory Management

- View all directories across the platform
- Disable or remove directories that violate terms of service
- View member counts and activity

### Business Listings

- Approve, edit, or remove business listing submissions
- Add business listings on behalf of partners
- Manage categories and featured listings

### Audit Log

The audit log (`/admin/audit-logs`) records all sensitive admin operations:
- Who performed the action (actor user ID)
- What action was taken
- Which entity was affected
- When (UTC timestamp)
- From which IP address and user-agent

This log is immutable — entries cannot be edited or deleted — and is required for SOC 2 compliance.

## Privacy & Your Data

### What Data We Collect

- **Account data**: email, full name, phone number, password (stored as a bcrypt hash — never in plaintext)
- **Profile data**: city, employer, industry, organization affiliations, profile photo
- **Usage data**: timestamps of logins, message reads, and profile views within directories
- **Consent records**: timestamps of when you agreed to each version of our Privacy Policy, Terms, and Sensitive Data consent

### Sensitive Personal Data

Your participation in Greek Orthodox community life — parish membership, organization affiliations, directory memberships — is classified as **sensitive personal data** under:
- California Consumer Privacy Act / CPRA
- 20 US state privacy laws
- PIPEDA (Canada)
- Quebec Law 25
- GDPR (EU)

You gave explicit consent to this classification when you registered. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

### How Your Data Is Protected

- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (cost 12) — your plaintext password is never stored
- Authentication tokens are stored securely: Keychain on iOS, EncryptedSharedPreferences on Android, Secure cookies on web
- All API traffic is encrypted using TLS
- Religious community data is never sold or shared with third-party advertisers
- No cross-site tracking pixels or third-party analytics embedded in the app

### Data Retention

- Active account data: retained while your account is active
- Deleted account data: permanently deleted 30 days after account deletion request
- Email logs: retained for 90 days for deliverability troubleshooting
- Audit logs: retained for 7 years (SOC 2 requirement)

### Your Rights

| Right | How to Exercise |
|---|---|
| Right to know (CCPA §1798.110) | Download your data from Account Settings |
| Right to delete (CCPA §1798.105, GDPR Art. 17) | Delete your account from Account Settings |
| Right to correct | Edit your profile at any time |
| Opt-out of sensitive data processing | Delete your account |
| Privacy questions / complaints | Email [privacy@hellenicdir.com](mailto:privacy@hellenicdir.com) |

## Troubleshooting

### I cannot sign in

1. Double-check your email address for typos
2. Click **Forgot Password** and follow the reset steps
3. Check that you verified your email when you first registered — unverified accounts cannot sign in

### I did not receive the verification email

1. Check your spam or junk folder
2. Look for an email from `noreply@hellenicdir.com`
3. If not found, try registering again with the same email — a new link will be sent
4. Contact [support@hellenicdir.com](mailto:support@hellenicdir.com) if the issue persists

### I was removed from a directory

Contact the administrator of the directory. You can find their name on the directory's contact information page.

### A business listing is incorrect or should be removed

Contact [support@hellenicdir.com](mailto:support@hellenicdir.com) with the business name and the issue.

### A parish is missing or has incorrect information

Parish data comes from GOARCH and is updated daily. If a parish is missing from GOARCH's records, it will not appear in this directory. Contact your diocesan office to update GOARCH's data.

### The app is slow or not loading data

1. Check your internet connection
2. On web, try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R / Cmd+R)
3. On iOS, close and reopen the app
4. On Android, close and reopen the app
5. If the issue persists, the service may be experiencing maintenance — check again in a few minutes

### My data export is empty or incomplete

Data exports include everything stored at the time of the request. If you have just registered, there may be little data yet. If data you expect to see is missing, contact [privacy@hellenicdir.com](mailto:privacy@hellenicdir.com).
